Welcome! This course takes you from zero design experience to a job-ready UX portfolio using Figma.

What is UX Design?

User Experience design is the process of designing products that are useful, usable, and enjoyable. It sits at the intersection of psychology, visual design, and technology.

Tip: You don't need to be a great illustrator to be a great UX designer. Logic and empathy matter more than drawing skill.
